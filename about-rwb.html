About RWB

"We create only one RWB Porsche for each customer"

RWB

RAUH Welt BEGRIFF stands for wide body kit tuning company specializing in Porsche made by Akira Nakai based in Japan.

Before tuning Porsche, it was a Japanese car tuner such as Toyota AE86 and Nissan Silvia. However, Nakai developed the first RWB Porsche build called 'Stella Artois' with the Porsche 930, becoming the company that only tune classical Porsche. It then released a kit from Classic 911 including 930 to last air-cooled 911, 993.

Every RWB vehicles all go through Nakai's hands. This is because of RWB's unique sales method, when you buy full-parts, Nakai takes them and puts them on his own. The mounting process takes about 2 to 3 days, with Akira Nakai alone.

The first RWB Porsche of each country is produced by Nakai with a concept that suits each country if the Porsche owner wants to. For example, "Sopranos," China's first official building, is the "RWB of the Chinese Mafia" concept with a color combination of black body, gold wheel and red spoiler, while Hong Kong's first official building, "Tiffany," is a color combination of mint body, rose gold wheel and black spoiler, "Lightening RWB in the Hong Kong Night."

Cars built to test new wheels or new body kits, spoilers, and canards are called RWB official builds.
